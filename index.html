About Us

The Fairhaven Christadelphian Charitable Foundation was formed in 1985 following the sale of Fairhaven House – a retirement residence in Toronto that had provided a Christ centered environment for Christadelphian seniors since 1957. The proceeds from the sale of the building were used to fund the Foundation.

The Foundation's primary mandate is to provide grants to Canadian Christadelphian ecclesias to support their seniors who may need help in affording suitable accommodation or assistance with other health and welfare needs. A secondary mandate is to assist members of the wider Canadian Christadelphian community who may also be in need of financial help as determined by their ecclesia.
